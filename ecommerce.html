Borderlands Mexico: Port of Brownsville completes $295M ship channel deepening project

The Port of Brownsville completed a $295.2 million deepening project that expands its ship channel from 42 to 52 feet, enabling larger vessels and positioning the only Gulf-deepwater border port to compete for cargo previously barred by depth constraints. With 80% of Brownsville's trade flowing to Mexico, the infrastructure upgrade directly amplifies cross-border commerce capacity at a critical Texas-Mexico gateway.
