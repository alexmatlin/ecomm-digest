Amazon is closing Mechanical Turk to new customers, signaling a potential wind-down of its two-decade-old crowdsourced labor marketplace. Borderlands Mexico: Cargo theft falls, but attacks on truckers turn deadlier

Cargo theft investigations in Mexico fell 21% to 2,099 cases in the first five months of 2026 versus 2,653 in the same period last year, but trucking industry groups report at least 14 driver deaths from increasingly violent attacks using firearms. The shift signals organized crime groups are abandoning stealth theft for aggressive highway robberies, with the State of Mexico, Veracruz-Puebla corridor, and Guanajuato identified as highest-risk zones where criminals now fire on trucks to force stops rather than wait for voluntary surrender.
