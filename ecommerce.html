DHL transitions Zelostech autonomous vehicles to live Singapore hub ops

DHL has moved Zelostech autonomous vehicles from pilot to daily operations at its Singapore hub, deploying fully electric driverless shuttles for point-to-point facility transfers that operate 24/7 at roughly half the cost of diesel trucks. The shift signals a meaningful pivot toward autonomous last-mile logistics in regional hubs, where repetitive, controlled movements can justify the technology.
